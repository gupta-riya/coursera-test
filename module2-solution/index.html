OUR MENU
PASTA

Pasta is an Italian dish typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

CHOWMEIN

Chowmein is a chinese dish made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched or extruded, into long strips or strings. This is usually cooked in boiling water, sometimes with cooking oil or salt added.

SAMOSA

Samosa is an Indian dish and is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese, beef and other meats, or lentils.
